Driven by Passion

Velocity X is a team of dedicated students from the UAE, competing in the F1 in Schools challenge. We blend cutting-edge technology, innovative design, and sheer determination to build the fastest miniature F1 car. Our journey is fueled by a passion for STEM and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Engineered for Speed

Our car is the culmination of hundreds of hours of research, design, and manufacturing. Utilizing advanced aerodynamics, lightweight materials, and precision engineering, every component is optimized for one thing: pure velocity.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) optimized body
  • 3D-printed components for complex geometries
  • Ultra-low friction bearing and axle system
  • State-of-the-art virtual wind tunnel testing
